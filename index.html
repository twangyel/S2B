FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse any Indian shopping site, copy the product link and paste it into the order form above. You can also upload screenshots if you don't have a direct link.

How do payments work?

After you submit the form, we contact you on WhatsApp to confirm product availability, total price (including service charge), and payment. Orders are placed only after payment is confirmed.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are fulfilled weekly. Typical end-to-end time is 5–10 days from order confirmation, depending on the seller's shipping speed within India.

Can I send screenshots instead of links?

Yes, absolutely. Use the file upload field in the form to attach product screenshots. You can also send them directly in the WhatsApp chat after submitting.

What items can't be delivered?

Restricted, prohibited, or oversized items may not be eligible. Items like flammable goods, large furniture, or commercially restricted products cannot be shipped. Contact us on WhatsApp if you're unsure about a specific item.

How is the service charge calculated?

Our service charge is tiered: 15% for orders under ₹2,000, 10% for ₹2,000–₹5,999, and 8% for ₹6,000 and above. Use the estimator at the top of this page to get a quick idea of the total before ordering.
