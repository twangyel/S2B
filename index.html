FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering with us.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 7-14 business days from confirmation. This includes procurement from India, customs clearance at the border, and final delivery to your address in Bhutan. We'll keep you updated via WhatsApp at every stage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Bank Transfer (direct deposit) and Mobile Payment (mBoB, MPay, and other Bhutan mobile banking apps). Payment is only requested after we confirm the final quote including exact shipping costs.

Are there any items you cannot deliver?

We cannot deliver prohibited items including weapons, explosives, flammable materials, perishable goods, and items restricted by Bhutan customs regulations. If unsure, send us the link and we'll verify before you place the order.

How is the service fee calculated?

Our service fee is tiered: 15% for orders under ₹2,000, 10% for orders between ₹2,000-₹5,999, and 8% for orders above ₹6,000. This covers our procurement, handling, and coordination costs. Use our Fee Estimator to calculate your total.

What if my product arrives damaged?

We inspect all packages before handover. If an item arrives damaged due to shipping, contact us immediately via WhatsApp with photos. We'll work with you and the seller to resolve the issue — refunds or replacements depend on the store's policy.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed and we've procured your items, we share tracking updates via WhatsApp. You can also visit our Order Portal with your Order ID to check real-time status anytime.
