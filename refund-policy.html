Refund Policy

Our commitment to fairness and transparency on every order.

Last updated: 23 May 2026

Overview

At Shop2Bhutan, we act as your personal procurement and delivery partner for products sourced from Indian e-commerce platforms. Because we place orders on your behalf after payment confirmation, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both parties while acknowledging the unique nature of cross-border personal shopping.

All refund requests must be submitted via WhatsApp within the specified timeframes. Requests made after the deadline will not be eligible for refund or exchange.

Orders That Cannot Be Refunded

  • Confirmed and placed orders — Once we have placed the order with the Indian seller after your payment confirmation, the order cannot be cancelled or refunded.
  • Service fees — Our service charge (8–15% of product price) is non-refundable in all cases, as it covers our procurement effort, communication, and logistics coordination.
  • India-to-Jaigaon shipping — Domestic Indian shipping costs paid to the seller are non-refundable once the item has been dispatched.
  • Perishable, intimate, or custom-made items — These categories are final sale due to hygiene and custom-order nature.
  • Items damaged due to customer mishandling after delivery.

Eligible Refund Situations

1. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged, defective, or materially different from the product description:

  • You must report the issue via WhatsApp within 24 hours of delivery.
  • Clear photo and video evidence of the damage/packaging must be provided.
  • We will coordinate with the Indian seller for a replacement or refund.
  • If the seller accepts a return, we will process a refund for the product price (excluding service fee) within 7–14 business days after the seller refunds us.

2. Wrong Item Delivered

If the item received is completely different from what was ordered (wrong product, wrong size, or wrong colour due to seller error):

  • Report within 24 hours of delivery with photo evidence.
  • We will attempt to exchange the item with the seller at no extra product cost.
  • If exchange is not possible, a partial refund of the product price may be issued (service fee excluded).
  • Return shipping to India will be borne by us only if the error was the seller's fault.

3. Item Not Delivered

If an item is lost in transit and never reaches us in Jaigaon:

  • We will file a claim with the Indian seller / courier.
  • Once the seller refunds us, we will refund you the full product price.
  • The service fee will be held as a credit toward your next order.

Refund Timeline

  • Approval: Within 2 business days of receiving your request and evidence.
  • Processing: 7–14 business days after approval (depends on Indian seller response time).
  • Payment method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method (bank transfer or mobile wallet).

Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Change of mind after order placement.
  • Minor cosmetic variations (colour shade differences within reasonable range).
  • Size issues where the customer selected the wrong size.
  • Delays caused by Indian seller shipping or customs inspection.
  • Products that are restricted, prohibited, or seized by customs (customer assumes this risk).

How to Request a Refund

Send a WhatsApp message to +975 77113302 with:

  • Your Order ID
  • Clear description of the issue
  • Photos / videos of the item and packaging

We aim to respond to all refund inquiries within 24 hours during business days.
